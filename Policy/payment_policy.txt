PAYMENT POLICY

1. Accepted Payment Methods
- UPI, Wallets, Debit/Credit Cards, Net Banking, EMI, COD.

2. COD Restrictions
- COD not available for high-value orders.
- COD fee may apply for certain pincode locations.

3. EMI Rules
- EMI is available for orders above ₹3,000.
- EMI interest depends on the issuing bank.

4. Failed Payment Handling
- If money is debited but order not created, amount is refunded within 3–5 business days.
- If payment fails repeatedly, try another payment method or contact support.

5. Payment Security
- All transactions are encrypted and follow RBI compliance guidelines.

6. Verification
- High-value transactions may require OTP or two-factor authentication.
