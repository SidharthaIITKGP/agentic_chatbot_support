CANCELLATION POLICY

1. Cancellation Window
- Orders can be cancelled before they are packed or shipped.
- Once shipped, cancellation is not guaranteed and depends on courier status.

2. Charges on Cancellation
- No cancellation fee for orders cancelled before packaging.
- If the order is already shipped, customers may need to refuse delivery.

3. Instant Refund for Cancellation
- For prepaid orders cancelled before shipment, refund is initiated instantly.

4. How to Cancel
- Go to My Orders → Select Item → Tap “Cancel Order”.

5. Special Cases
- Custom-made or perishable items cannot be cancelled once processed.
