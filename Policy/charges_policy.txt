CHARGES & FEES POLICY

1. Delivery Charges
- Standard delivery fee may apply for orders below the free-shipping threshold.
- Delivery fee is non-refundable once the order is shipped.

2. Convenience Fee
- Certain high-demand or priority items may include a small convenience fee.

3. COD Charges
- Cash on Delivery orders may have a service fee depending on location.

4. Pricing Variations
- Prices may change due to offers, promotions, or demand fluctuations.
- The price at checkout is final.

5. Taxes
- All prices are inclusive of GST unless stated otherwise.

6. Why Customers May See Extra Charges
- COD service charge
- Priority/express delivery fee
- Packaging or handling fee for fragile items
